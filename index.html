My Coding Journey



College

I obtained my Bachelor's of Science degree in Sociology (with a minor in Psychology). At the time of graduating, I was interested in going into the
Social Service / NonProfit route. I have always been super interested in tech, but always thought that it was "too difficult" to do as a full time career.





Early Career

My first full-time job out of college was as a Social Worker for a third party organization that worked with the Department of Family Services.
I stayed there for 9 months, where my husband and I moved back to St Louis. When we relocated, I got a position as a Teaching Assistant at an elementary school.
I really enjoyed helping people, but I liked the aspect of working 1:1 with children.




Learning More

March 2018, I realized I was very interested in transitioning careers. I began by taking some online courses online, such as SQL, data analysis,
and Python. Those types of courses really peaked my interests. Right then and there, I KNEW I wanted to have a career in programming.
Over those next few months, I spent a lot of my free time learning and taking different online courses. Unfortunately, without a computer science degree,
I was not sure how I would take this knowledge and find a career with it.





Launchcode

I found out about Launchcode early into Fall and thought it was such a cool program. Once applications opened, I applied. I was so ready
and excited, but unfortunately the application window was open for almost two months before accepting any applications. Once I got accepted, I was
SO THRILLED. It was such an exciting step and moment in my life that I knew I was where I needed to be. Launchcode not only opens so many doors for people,
it also brings in such a great community of individuals togethers. It gives such an opportunity that others might not feel was possible before.
